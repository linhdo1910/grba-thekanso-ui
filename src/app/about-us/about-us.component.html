Brand story

The KANSO was founded in 2024 out of a passion for Japanese design philosophy minimal yet sophisticated, harmonious yet functional. We believe that a neat and simplified living space not only creates an elegant aesthetic but also brings peace and balance to the soul. Inspired by the "Kanso" principle in wabi-sabi, which emphasizes simplicity and the natural essence of materials, The KANSO aims to offer furniture that helps customers create a serene, modern living environment infused with the essence of Japanese culture.

Vision & Mission

The KANSO is committed to delivering minimalist, refined, and sustainable furniture, helping customers build harmonious living spaces, reduce clutter, and enhance their quality of life. To become the leading Japanese-style furniture brand in Vietnam and the region, providing people with simplified, aesthetic, and nature-inspired living space solutions.

Core Values

Minimalism & Functionality: Every product from The KANSO is designed with a spirit of simplicity while ensuring optimal functionality.
Quality & Sustainability: We select natural, eco-friendly materials and meticulously craft our products to ensure longevity.
Harmony & Inspiration: A living space is not just a place to reside but also a source of inspiration and soul nourishment, helping individuals find balance in life.
Customer-Centric Approach: Each product from The KANSO is designed to enhance user experience, ensuring comfort and convenience in every detail.
